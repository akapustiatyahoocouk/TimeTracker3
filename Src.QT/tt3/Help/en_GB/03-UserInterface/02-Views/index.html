Views

A "view" is a visual way to organize a certain activity; common examples are "manage accounts", "enter new tasks that can be worked on", "record work and enter comments" or "view quick summary of the current working day".

TimeTracker3 comes with a predefined set of views, most of which are accessible from every skin. These are:

  • Users/Accounts view.
  • Activity Types view.
  • Public Activities view.
  • Public Tasks view.
  • Private Activities view.
  • Private Task view.
  • Projects view.
  • Work Streams view.
  • Beneficiaries view.
  • My Day view.
  • Quick Reports view.

The means of accessing a specific view vary depending on the skin. For example, in "Administrator" skin all views are presented as tabs in a single  window where user can switch between the views by selecting the required tab or pressing the corresponding hotkey, while in "Sklim" skin all views are available as modal popup dialogs.
