Welcome to TimeTracker3

TimeTracker3 is a tool that helps every software engineer to keep track of their activities during the week, the month,... always.

Ever wondered how to file a "working hours" report at the end of the week? How would you remember on Friday just how much time you've spent on that pointless meeting on Monday?

Have no fear, TimeTracker3 is here.

With just one click TimeTracker3 will record the time and effort you've spent on any given activity or task, providing:

  • A minute-accurate report of your activities during the week.
  • A live overview of your daily efforts and what they are contributing to.
  • A guide on how to manage your time better.

Just imagine a chess clock with multiple buttons - once you start working on something, press the corresponding button; TimeTracker3 will do the rest.
