TimeTracker3 concepts

TimeTracker3 is organised around a basic set of concepts, such as:

  • Users and Accounts - these define accessibility and access rights for different peoiple within an organisation.
  • Activities and tasks - these are things people can be "working on", and it is the time spent on these activities and tasks that TimeTracker3 records.
  • Workflows and beneficiaries - TODO
  • Live reports - TimeTracker3 allows users to see their daily progress with their activities and tasks (e.g. how much time was dedicated to meetings? how much to their current project? or a mundane lunch break?...and so on.)
  • Reports - TimeTracker3 will, when asked to, generate aggregated reports on the workers' performance - how much time was dedicated to each project, how that benefits the projects' target audiences and whom to bill for what.
