Beneficiaries

In TimeTracker3 a Beneficiary represents an external entity who "benefits from" the effort spent (and recorded) by the team members. Ultimately, these are the entities who are billed for the team members' working time.

For a Beneficiary, the following information is kept:

  • Name - a short descriptive name identifying the specific Beneficiary.
  • Description - a longer description, providing additional information on what a specific Beneficiary is.
  • Project links - by associating Beneficiaries with Projects TimeTracker3 keeps track on how much time and effort was dedicated to a specific Beneficiary (and must, therefore, be booked as expenses towards that Beneficiary).
